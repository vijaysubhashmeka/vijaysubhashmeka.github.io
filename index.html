// Who I Am

About Me

I'm Vijay Subhash Meka, an Electronics and Communication Engineer driven by a passion for creating, teaching, and innovating. My journey started with circuits and microcontrollers, and has evolved into a full vision for the future of technology.

From Arduino-based projects to complex robotic arms, I bring hands-on engineering knowledge to every challenge. My goal is not just to solve problems — but to document and share every solution so others can learn from it.

I believe engineering education should be accessible, practical, and inspiring. That's why I create content, write documentation, and provide project support — turning theory into reality.
